Async-IO: Training

Learn from the Experts!
Training will guide you through the Atmosphere Framework. It’s an in-depth instructor-led training course with case-discussion held by Atmosphere developers

Description

The course aims to provide a solid foundation in Comet/WebSockets Protocol, starting with basic fundamental concepts and internals throughout best-practices, key features and asynchronous I/O with The Atmosphere Framework. During and after the training hours there will be time for discussions as well as attendee case-studies. At the end of the training you will have a deep understanding of how Atmosphere Framework works, you will be able to reliably analyze, understand and solve common problems and ready to build state of a scalable WebSocket/Comet applications.

Course Description

  • Introduction - Terminology, basic concepts, setting up and basic operations
  • Server side - Picking the right module based on your needs.
  • Client side - Picking the right client library based on your needs.
  • Distributed Model - Atmosphere cluster, shards and replicas, configuration, APIs and local gateway
  • Scaling Atmosphere - Picking the right server, configuring the server, proxy and firewall
